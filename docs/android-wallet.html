Android Wallet

Warning

This is a beta version of mobile wallet. Do not use on a phone or device with sensitive or important personal data. Use at your own risk.

The beta version of Android wallet for Mainnet and Testnet T4 has been released (download). Do not send any Ethereum coins or DogeP tokens or other coins to this wallet. It is only meant for Quantum Coin mainnet and T4 Testnet. It is recommended to try only on Testnet T4 till release version becomes available.

To install this wallet, you will need to enable APK installation in your phone or device.

https://github.com/quantumcoinproject/quantum-coin-wallet-android/releases/download/v2.0.13/QuantumCoin-wallet.apk
